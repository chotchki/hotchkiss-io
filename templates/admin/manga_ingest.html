Bulk manga ingest

Manga library →

Turn a folder of .epub or .cbz files into an ordered stack of volume pages under a series. Each file becomes one book page (its cover is pulled from the book automatically); the volume number is read from the filename (v012, Vol 12, c001, #12). The series is created if it doesn't exist yet, gated Family like the rest of the library. Re-running is safe — a volume already ingested is skipped.
